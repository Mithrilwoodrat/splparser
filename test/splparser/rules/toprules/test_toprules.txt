
Below are clips from an interactive session in which I test various aspects of the parser.

>>> import splparser.parser

>>> splparser.parse("top blockedUrl").print_tree()
('ROOT')
    ('STAGE')
        ('TOP')
            ('WORD': 'blockedUrl')

>>> splparser.parse("top 10 signature").print_tree()
('ROOT')
    ('STAGE')
        ('TOP')
            ('INT': '10')
            ('WORD': 'signature')

>>> splparser.parse("top limit=10000 msize,Drive_Type by host").print_tree()
('ROOT')
    ('STAGE')
        ('TOP')
            ('EQ')
                ('LIMIT')
                ('INT': '10000')
            ('WORD': 'msize')
            ('ID': 'Drive_Type')
            ('BY')
                ('WORD': 'host')
